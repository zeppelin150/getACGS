About Cambric AI

Building the tools organizations need to understand and govern their AI systems.

AI systems are becoming central to how organizations operate — but the tooling to monitor, understand, and govern those systems hasn't kept up. Cambric AI exists to close that gap.

We build practical, outcome-driven tools: security monitoring that adapts to your environment, operational analytics that surface what matters, and governance infrastructure for the AI systems you rely on.

Our team combines experience in AI research, cybersecurity, and enterprise software. We're focused on building the kind of reliable, unglamorous infrastructure that organizations actually need as they adopt AI at scale.

Our principles

  • Build for outcomes, not demos
  • Security and reliability by default
  • Transparent and auditable systems
  • Research-informed, product-focused
  • Governance that scales with autonomy

Enterprise DNA

We build for production environments. Our tools are designed for the teams actually running AI systems — not just evaluating them.

Research-Driven

We publish and present at conferences, keeping our products grounded in the latest work in AI safety, behavioral analytics, and neural architectures.

Long-Term Vision

We're building toward the governance infrastructure that autonomous systems will require — starting with the practical tools organizations need today.
